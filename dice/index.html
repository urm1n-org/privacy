Data Collection and Usage

Random Tools does not collect, store, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information.

Tool preferences and settings are processed and stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

The app does not require sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, or location to function.

Third-Party Services

Random Tools is built using React Native and Expo. While the app itself does not collect data, platform providers such as the Apple App Store or Google Play may process anonymous diagnostics under their own policies.

Children's Privacy

Since no personal information is collected, the app is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated over time. Continued use of the app after an update means acceptance of the revised policy.
