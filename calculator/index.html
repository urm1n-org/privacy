Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Calculator app ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII).

All calculations, history, and settings (such as theme preferences and calculator modes) are processed and stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

2. Permissions

The app does not require any special permissions (such as camera, microphone, or location) to function.

3. Third-Party Services

This app is built using React Native and Expo. While the app itself does not collect data, the underlying platform or app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store) may collect anonymous usage statistics or crash reports governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, our app is safe for use by children of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, email us at urmin.org+support@gmail.com.
